WELCOME TO THE MUSHROOMS STUDY!

Overview

This study will take approximately 40 minutes and will be completed entirely on Inquisit.

On the next page, you will find the consent form. You will first go over some instructions and a few practice rounds before you officially start to play the game. Once you begin, you will need to complete everything before exiting Inquisit. We will redirect you back to the Prolific page after you submit. At that time, you may delete the Inquisit application from your device.

If you wish to withdraw your participation at any point during the study, please

press Cntl + Q on your keyboard to skip through all the tasks and return to Prolific (you may have to press the keys multiple times to exit Inquisit). Note that you will not receive the full payment on Prolific if you withdraw before completing all parts of the study.

Payment

After you finish all tasks on Inquisit, you will automatically receive a payment of approximately $8 for your time on Prolific. Once we review your data, you may also receive a bonus payment based on your performance.

Thank you for participating in the Mushrooms study!
